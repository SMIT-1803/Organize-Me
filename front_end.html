Organize Me

  • Scroll down to the “Approved Integrations” or look for a button labeled “+ New Access Token” (depends on your institution’s Canvas setup)
  • Click “+ New Access Token”
  • Give it a Purpose name (e.g., "My App" or "Personal Script")
  • (Optional) Set an expiration date if you want it to expire automatically
  • Click “Generate Token”
  • Copy the token (API key) that appears — you won't be able to see it again after you leave the page, so save it securely.
